About Me

My name is Yannan Tang, a Ph.D. Candidate in statistics at University of California, Irvine. My research focuses on statistical methodologies for individual-level geospatial data analysis using generalized additive models and relevant generalizations, with application on spatial epidemiology studies.

With great honor, I would like to name my advisor Professor Daniel Gillen and our collaborators, Professor Veronica Viera and Professor Scott Bartel.
