About Me

Yannan Tang (汤岩楠), Ph.D. in statistics from University of California, Irvine. My academic work focuses on statistical methodologies for individual-level geospatial data analysis using generalized additive models and relevant generalizations, with application in spatial epidemiology studies. With great honor, here I name my academic advisor, Professor Daniel Gillen and our collaborators, Professor Veronica Viera and Professor Scott Bartel.

Since 2020, I have been working as a statistical scientist at Genentech, where I support late phase clinical trials in molecule development for ophthalmology disease treatment.
